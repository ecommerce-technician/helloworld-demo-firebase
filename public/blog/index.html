What is Bundles Lang?

To put it simply bundleslang makes asking big questions easier. It is a simple querying language for getting information out of the internet and then automates whatever at the end.

Example Bundles Lang queries in Finance

Theranos
select * from twitter, news where keyword contains theranos limit to 50 strings Theranos mentions in San Francisco select * from twitter where keyword contains disney limit to 30 strings around 37.781157,-122.398720,7mi geocode
